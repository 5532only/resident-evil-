Game Overview

The Ultimate Survival Racing Experience

Survival Karts thrusts you into a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, where the only escape is through high-speed kart racing. This isn't your ordinary racing game - it's a fight for survival where speed, strategy, and firepower determine who lives and who joins the undead.

Developed by acclaimed game studio Apocalypse Games, Survival Karts combines the adrenaline rush of kart racing with intense zombie survival elements. With stunning graphics, responsive controls, and immersive gameplay, it's garnered over 500,000 players worldwide since its release.
